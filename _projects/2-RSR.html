RSR (Regression and Successive Rejects)

Efficient hyperparameter optimization methods are crucial for current machine learning problems since evaluating different configurations is expensive. Popular hyperparameter tuning methods such as Bayesian Optimization perform well in low-dimensional (n < 5) scenarios yet gradually become slower as n increases. Similarly, algorithms dependent on random search and early-stopping, although relatively quick, do not prove to be as robust. RSR uses multi-armed bandits and regression to better predict stronger configurations in shorter periods.
