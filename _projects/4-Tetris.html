Tetris

As part of the Data Structures: Honors course at UT Austin, one of the projects was to build a tetris clone. This Tetris clone had to have all the functionalities of the original Tetris game, including shifts, rotations, colored blocks. Additionally, the game was created with wall jumps, a more advanced feature of Tetris. Furthermore, I created a genetic algorithm capable of playing the game successfully.
