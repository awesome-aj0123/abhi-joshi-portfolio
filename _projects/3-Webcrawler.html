Webcrawler

As part of the Data Structures: Honors course at UT Austin, one of the projects was to build a webcrawler. As an example, Google’s search engine is one of the most notable public Webcrawlers. Similar to Google’s webcrawler, our webcrawler had to have the ability to search through large amounts files in a managable time-frame. Using the knowledge I had gained throughout the semester, I successfully completed the project.
